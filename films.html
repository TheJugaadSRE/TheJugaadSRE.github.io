Why Filmmaking Matters for DevRel

Great developer advocacy is fundamentally about storytelling — making the complex feel human, the technical feel approachable, and the abstract feel real. The same instincts that go into writing a film script — clarity of message, emotional arc, respect for your audience's time — are exactly what makes technical content land.

Filmmaking taught me to show, not tell. That principle shapes every demo, every talk, and every piece of content I create.
